Artificial Intelligence + Cybersecurity + X

Securing Intelligent Systems Across Emerging Frontiers

AICYBEX Lab advances research at the intersection of artificial intelligence and cybersecurity. The X dimension represents emerging domains such as cyberbiosecurity, healthcare AI, cyber-physical systems, autonomous systems, and future intelligent technologies.

Core Research Model

AI + Cybersecurity + X

AI and cybersecurity form the core of AICYBEX. The X dimension expands our work into high-impact emerging domains where intelligent technologies create new security, privacy, safety, and resilience challenges.

AI

AI Security

LLM security, AI red teaming, jailbreak detection, backdoor attacks, adversarial machine learning, and trustworthy AI.

CYBER

Cybersecurity

Network security, intrusion detection and response, attack graphs, threat intelligence, and AI-powered cyber defense.

X

Emerging Domains

Cyberbiosecurity, Bio-LLMs, healthcare AI, cyber-physical systems, autonomous systems, and future frontiers.
