Artificial Intelligence + Cybersecurity + X

Securing Intelligent Systems Across Emerging Frontiers

AICYBEX Lab is a research group dedicated to advancing the security, trustworthiness, and resilience of intelligent systems. Our research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and cybersecurity, with a focus on protecting next-generation technologies from emerging threats. We develop innovative methods for AI security, network and system defense, cyber-physical systems security, and the protection of biological and biomedical AI systems.

Core Research Model

AI + Cybersecurity + X

AI and cybersecurity form the core of AICYBEX. The X dimension expands our work into high-impact emerging domains where intelligent technologies create new security, privacy, safety, and resilience challenges.
