Securing Intelligent Systems Across Emerging Frontiers

AICYBEX Lab advances research at the intersection of artificial intelligence and cybersecurity, where X represents emerging domains including cyberbiosecurity, healthcare AI, cyber-physical systems, autonomous systems, and future intelligent technologies.

Core Research Model

AI + Cybersecurity + X

AI and Cybersecurity form the foundation of AICYBEX. The X dimension expands our work into high-impact emerging domains.

AI

Artificial Intelligence

Trustworthy AI, LLM security, AI red teaming, adversarial machine learning, and intelligent defense.

X

Emerging Domains

Cyberbiosecurity, healthcare AI, CPS, robotics, autonomous systems, and future frontiers.

CYBER

Cybersecurity

Network security, intrusion detection and response, attack graphs, threat intelligence, and cyber defense.
