Research Vision

AI and Cybersecurity form the core of AICYBEX. The X dimension expands our work into high-impact emerging domains.

AI Security

LLM security, AI red teaming, jailbreak detection, backdoor attacks, trustworthy and robust AI.

Cybersecurity

Network security, intrusion detection and response, attack graphs, cyber defense, and threat intelligence.

X Domains

Cyberbiosecurity, Bio-LLMs, healthcare AI, cyber-physical systems, autonomous systems, and future technologies.
